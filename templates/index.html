Empowering Transparency: SALNQuire digitizes accountability for a corruption-free tomorrow.



SALNQuire is an academic initiative by second-year students from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP). This project focuses on developing a comprehensive Database Management System (DBMS) that mirrors the government's Statement of Assets, Liabilities, and Net Worth (SALN) form. By meticulously replicating the data fields and reporting requirements of the SALN form, students aim to gain hands-on experience in database management and system development. Digitalizing the SALN is crucial for effective data querying and strict enforcement. It ensures public officials are held accountable for their assets and liabilities, playing a vital role in combating corruption.
